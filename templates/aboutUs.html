Welcome to Smart Agriculture System...

Smart Agriculture System provides data-driven approach to farming, enabling farmers to make informed decisions, reduce wastage, and enhance overall efficiency. The need for such a system arises from the increasing demand for food production, coupled with the cahllenges posed by climate change and depleting natural resources. Traditional farming methods face limitations. Here, sensors collect the data. Arduino Uno acts as the central processing unit. The collected data is then transmitted to a real-time analyser. The insights derived are then sent to dashboard.
